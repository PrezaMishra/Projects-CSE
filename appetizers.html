  • PANEER TIKKA

    This North Indian tikka variety consists of a combination of marinated paneer cheese and vegetables that are cooked in a tandoor - a traditional Indian oven. This vegetarian version of tikka is based on paneer, a crumbly cheese with a soft texture.

  • GOBI MANCHURIAN

    Gobi manchurian is a traditional Indo-Chinese dish consisting of fried cauliflower (gobi) that’s tossed in a sweet and spicy sauce (manchurian). There are two versions of the dish – dry and in gravy.

  • KOSAMBARI

    Kosambari is the most popular salad from the Indian state of Karnataka. The salad is made with split legumes such as bengal gram and green gram, and it is additionally seasoned with mustard seeds.

  • KURKURI BHINDI

    Kurkuri bhindi recipe – thinly sliced okra are coated and marinated with besan and spices. Then deep fried into the hot oil till crisp. At the end, sprinkling of chaat masala adds another flavor punch.

Non-Vegetarian

  • Chicken Tikka


    Chicken tikka is a popular Indian dish consisting of small, boneless pieces of chicken marinated in a yogurt and spice blend, then grilled on skewers, typically in a tandoor oven, giving it a smoky flavor.

  • Chicken Seekh Kebab


    Minced meat mixed with spices and herbs, shaped into skewers and grilled. A smoky, succulent dish perfect for appetizers.

  • Fish Amritsari


    Spicy fish fillets coated in a gram flour batter and deep-fried to crispy perfection. A popular, tangy seafood snack from Punjab.

  • Prawn Koliwada


    Prawns marinated in spices and deep-fried in a crispy batter. A crunchy, flavorful appetizer often served with chutney.
